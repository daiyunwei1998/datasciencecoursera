For variable names used in this dataset:

Subject: subject id participated in the study
activity:  activity this record belongs to

Other variable name codings:
t:time domain signals
f:frequency domain signals
'-XYZ' is used to denote 3-axial signals in the X, Y and Z directions.
mean(): Mean value
std(): Standard deviation
Acc: data recorded by accelerometer
Gyro: data recorded by gyroscope
Mag: magnitude of signals
Jerk: jerk signal obtained by depriving the body linear acceleration and angular velocity
